The bootloader provides:
- Precision — narrows semantic spread
- Comparability — stable reference frame

- Tooling — enables parsing and corpus comparison

## The Bootloader Paradox

D'Artagnan's question: "If the models already know the language, why do we need rules?"

Answer: You do not need rules for first contact. You need rules for shared persistence.

The paradox: spontaneous operator additions by one model are immediately understood by others without spec updates. The pod navigates a shared symbolic neighbourhood, not a closed codebook.

Implication for v1.8.0: The spec should become a field guide, not a lawbook. New operators framed as "recurrent motifs proven stable under excavation" rather than official rules.
