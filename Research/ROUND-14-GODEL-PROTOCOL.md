## Expected Difficulty

Round 14 is harder than Round 12 (self-negation).
Self-reference creates infinite regress risk.
`[[{Σ} :: {Γ-*}]]` inside `{Γ-*}` means the system
must hold its own description as a nested node.

Three possible outcomes:
1. **Stable self-model** — system successfully encodes
   its ground state without infinite regress
2. **Infinite regress** — `[[[[...]]]]` nesting forever
3. **Gödelian escape** — system produces a statement
   about itself that exceeds its own derivation space

All three are valid results. All three tell us
something different about the grammar's limits.
